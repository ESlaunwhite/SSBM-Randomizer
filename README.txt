Super Smash Bros. Melee Character Randomizer

Random's a character for you based on the characters that you choose.

♦ How to open:
	Open 'ssbm-randomizer.html' in a web browser
	
♦ How to use:
	Click on any/all characters you would like to random from 
	or
	Click on one of the 2 buttons to generate a random character from predetermined lists.
